Tour Plan

Overview

Embark on a captivating journey with the 4 Days Golden Triangle Tour Package offered by Incredible Trip.

This meticulously curated tour explores the cultural treasures of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, allowing you to witness majestic landmarks like the iconic Taj Mahal, magnificent forts, and vibrant markets. With expert guides and seamless transportation, Incredible Trip ensures a memorable and hassle-free experience, making it the perfect choice for those seeking a blend of luxury and cultural immersion.

Tour Itinerary

Start your trip with a pickup in your private air-conditioned car from your desired location in Delhi, Gurgaon, or Noida. Visit the famous Qutub Minar, a UNESCO World Heritage Site built in the 11th century. Then, head towards Bahai Temple, also known as Lotus Temple, and India Gate. Drive past the parliament house, the president's house, and other secretariat buildings. After the half-day Delhi city tour, head to Agra, the city of Taj Mahal, via a 3-hour drive on the Express Way. Upon your arrival in Agra, check in to your hotel and relax.

Visit Taj Mahal early in the morning to see the spectacular view of sun rising along with Taj Mahal, built by Mughal Emperor Shahjahan in 1630 in the memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz. After being mesmerized by the view, return to your hotel for breakfast. Then head towards the Agra Fort, accompanied by a guide. On the way to Jaipur, visit Fatehpur Sikri, also known as the ghost city. Have a look at the wonder in its architect. At the end of the drive, arrive in Jaipur and check in at your hotel.

After having breakfast, head towards the Amber Fort, the capital of the state until 1728. Go up to the hill by a decorated elephant, where your guide will take you on a tour of Amber Fort. After exploring the Amber Fort, visit Jal Mahal, a palace located in the middle of Man Sagar Lake, then visit Maharaja's city Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal, a large structure with a large number of windows. At the end of the day, return to your hotel.

After breakfast, relax in your air-conditioned private car, drive back to Delhi, and be dropped off at your desired location in Delhi, Gurgaon, or Noida. We assure you that you will have a lot of great memories of your 3-night, 4-days Golden Triangle tour.
