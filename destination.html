Tour Plan

Overview

Book a 3-Day Private Golden Triangle Tour Package with Incredible Trip to travel to India's most popular destinations. Experience the thrill of exploring these iconic locations with us. Our tour package includes beautiful and multicultural cities like Delhi, Agra and Jaipur. A Golden Triangle tour of these cities is an unforgettable journey through India's unique customs, culture and food. Our 3-day private tour of the Golden Triangle is very affordable and fits any budget.

This tour is the perfect solution to all your travel needs as it offers a combination of fun, culture, adventure and discovery. These three cities - Delhi, Agra and Jaipur - are unique in their own way from other Indian cities. They contribute immensely to the creation of an incredible India. During this 3-day trip, witness the serenity of the Taj Mahal and its heritage, Jaipur with its rich monuments that have deep connections to the construction and functioning of the city, and Delhi, the capital of India, where all food served to the people is rich in heart and makes every stranger feel at home.

Tour Itinerary

Our travel escort will assist you upon your arrival at the New Delhi airport and will facilitate your entry through the booked hotel, where you will rest for the night.

Depending on your arrival time, local visits to the Red Fort, India Gate, or Qutub Minar can be added to your itinerary. Ideally, with your group’s arrival in the morning, one of the monuments listed above can be easily seen. Shopping at the moonlit square, or Chandni Chowk, as it is known in the Hindi language, is worth a visit. The market is decorated, breathing, and alive all the time. As a complimentary addition to the 3-day private Golden Triangle tour, our tour escort will assist you throughout!

After an early morning breakfast, we will hit the roads towards Agra, the city of the Taj Mahal. As a standard offering of this Golden Triangle tour package, we provide our tourists with a top-notch vehicle that ferries them through the Yamuna Expressway that connects Delhi to Agra in 3 hours. Upon your arrival in Agra, our guide will assist you with check-in and refreshments thereafter.

We start exploring the erstwhile Mughal capital city at the epitome of love—the Taj Mahal. The gardens, platforms, domes, and minarets; the inlaid marble work (pachekari); the symmetry; the aesthetics; the mighty Yamuna on the backdrop—the Taj Mahal complex is nothing short of an exhilarating experience in its own right. In this 3-day private Golden Triangle tour of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, our tour guide will assist you through the monuments of the Red Fort in Agra and Itmad-ud-Daulah (also known as the Baby Taj, as its construction served as an inspiration for the Taj Mahal).

If time permits, spending the evening at the Mehtab Bagh on the opposite side of the Taj Mahal across the river Yamuna while the sun goes down is an exhilarating experience. We head towards Jaipur thereafter.

Upon arrival in Jaipur, the night is spent at the pre-booked hotel in Jaipur, which is also a standard offering of the Golden Triangle tour package and is covered in the 3-day private tour of the Golden Triangle.

Sightseeing in Jaipur starts after breakfast at the hotel in Jaipur. We start by visiting the majestic Amber Fort, along with the elephant ride. The amber fort campus has many wonders and structures worth visiting, like the Ganesh Pol, Sheesh Mahal, Chand Pol, Suraj Pol, Jalebi Chowk, Diwan-e-Aam, Diwan-e-Khaas, Jas Mandir, Sukh Niwas, Palace of Man Singh, and an open-air passage that connects it with the sister fort of Jaigarh. This last leg of the 3-day private Golden Triangle Tour is a living experience of the royalty that comes along with Rajputana valour and glory.

The subsequent visits to the Jal Mahal on the way back to the main Jaipur city, the City Palace (which in part houses the royalty and the remaining has been kept open for the public), and the Hawa Mahal are equally regal in feel and exuberant in nature. Though shopping at the decorated marketplaces of Jaipur takes another day, if time permits, the evening can be utilized in one of the most decorated and famous markets of Jaipur, the Bapu Bazar, where you may enjoy Rajasthani snacks along with the wonderful sculptures, art forms, jewelery, rugs, and glasswork!

This brings us to the end of the 3-day Private Golden Triangle Tour of India, after which we head straight to New Delhi in a vehicle arranged by us. Incredible Trip is a premium travel planner with more than 20 years of experience in this sector, committed to making your India travel awesome!
